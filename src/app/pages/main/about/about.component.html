Our Evolution

  • October 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Bluish Global was established in October 2019 under the ideology of returning profits to producers and consumers by reducing unnecessary intermediate trading processes occurring in the global fisheries industry, including Korea.

  • December 2019

    Take a leap forward

    Based on our old know-how in the fisheries industry and wide and reliable business relationships, we have gained opportunities as a distributor to handle under-valid products, laying the foundation for a comprehensive trading company that deals with various building materials, machinery, and raw materials in addition to fisheries.

  • July 2020

    Importing Live King Crab

    Our company's main item was selected as King Crab, and for the first time, Chile King Crab was imported and distributed in Korea. Our main suppliers are Norway Coast Seafoods, Norfra, Troika and Bergen. At the same time, frozen self-contained lobsters also recruited new domestic customers and started distribution.

  • November 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Starting with the COVID-19 era, our company started online sales of Live King Crab and Live Lobster to play a preemptive role in the seafood market.At the same time, Russian live king crab began to be exported to the Philippines, and it has been firmly maintaining its relationship with Filipino buyers for more than a year.

  • December 2021

    Forward to another Level

    With the aim of expanding the overseas market in earnest after two years of establishment, our company is busy supplying live Russian crab and Korean seafood to buyers around the world at reasonable prices, and at the same time signed contracts with domestic franchise companies to start distributing shrimp/lobsters to leap forward in 2022.
